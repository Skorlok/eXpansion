#Combined Localrecords and Dedimania records widget

When you connect to the server, you see on left LocalRecords widget with top30 times. This widget can show 
dedimania-times too, if the dedimania plugin is activated:

$oTo show dedimania times:
		1. click on the globe-icon next to "Local Records" text.

$oTo show local times:
		1. click on the globe-icon next to "Dedimania Records" text.


$oTo minimize or maximize the widget:
		1. click on the arrow -icon at end of Local Records-title bar.
